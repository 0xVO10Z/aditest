Refresher Courses

Been off the road for a while? Our refresher sessions help experienced drivers rebuild confidence, update knowledge and get back to their best.

  • No fixed lesson count — go at your pace
  • Focus on specific weak areas
  • Motorway driving covered
  • Post-pass confidence building

Test Prep

Mock Tests

Simulate the real DVSA driving test experience before the big day. Full test-condition drives to identify any remaining faults and reduce anxiety.

  • Real test route practice
  • Full debrief after each mock
  • Fault identification & correction
  • Test-day tips & mental prep

Moved to the UK with a foreign licence? We help overseas drivers adapt to UK roads, rules, and test requirements quickly and efficiently.

  • Theory & practical test support
